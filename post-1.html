Syndrome Imposter

by ntrovlupr on June 12, 2022

The way I handle imposter syndrome. In the beginning, I occasionally felt it, but now it rarely ever happens. whenever I start to feel it I tell myself. 1. "Relax it's normal, I'm feeling this way because I'm learning something new," 2. I think about what my coding coach says - "you can never go wrong learning to code", so take it all in, enjoy yourself, be a sponge! 3. I'll joke and say to myself that if that Frank Abagale figure can travel the world pretending to be an aviator and a doctor then with hard work and dedication to learn I can become a developer. whenever you feel doubt or "imposter syndrome" just remind yourself the positive aspects of what you are doing then get past it and continue to learn more. Never quit.
